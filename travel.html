Our Story

Guiding adventures since 2005

Who We Are

WildPath Adventures was founded in 2005 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for adventure with the world. What started as a small operation leading hiking trips in the Rockies has grown into an internationally recognized adventure travel company.

Our mission is to create unforgettable experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices. We believe in leaving no trace, supporting local communities, and fostering a deep appreciation for the natural world.
